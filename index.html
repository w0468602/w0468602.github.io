Assignment 3: Working with Images

A PNG is best for:
Clear images, logos, and charts. You can compress a png
and it will keep its quality. They support millions of
colors, animation, and transparency.
A JPG is best for:
They support millions of colors so they would be very clear
like a PNG, they would be good for everything that a PNG
is good for except they dont support transparency or animation.
A GIF is best for:
A GIF is best for animation, it supports less colors and
doesn't suppoprt transparency. They are not good for clear pictures.
